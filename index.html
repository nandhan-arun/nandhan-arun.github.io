About the Book

Welcome to my book of poems! As a student, I was captivated by the various forms of poetry we learned about in school, and that sparked a desire in me to create my own. Each poem in this book is a reflection of my personal experiences, thoughts, and emotions, expressed through the art of poetry.

With original pieces in various styles, from the whimsical limerick to the reflective autobiography poem, each one is a unique expression of my creativity and love for the written word. You'll also find examples of my handmade sketches, carefully chosen to complement and enhance the meaning of each poem.

Take a journey with me through the world of acrostic poems, haikus, diamantes, cinquains, and animal alliteration poems, and discover the beauty and power of poetry for yourself.
